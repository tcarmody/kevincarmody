KEVIN CARMODY

CIVIL RIGHTS AND TITLE IX CONSULTANT

Kevin Carmody is an experienced civil rights and Title IX investigator and coordinator with more than a decade of experience in the field. I am available to assist colleges and universities as a consultant to help write or edit policy to meet government regulations, shape practice to match their institutional values and mission, and train team members serving on Title IX cases. I can also be contracted to serve in advisory, investigative, counseling, and officer roles for specific cases.

Many organizations need additional staff to act as a Title IX Coordinator, Advisor, Investigator, Counselor, Hearing Officer, Appeals Officer, Informal Resolution Coordinator, or to work in Intake and Supportive Measures. I have served in all of these roles over my long career and am happy to train, supplement, or substitute for your existing staff.
